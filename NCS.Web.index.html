Street Biography

Born and raised in the tough neighborhoods of Mafambisa, Mpumalanga, NCS began his musical journey in 2002, using hip-hop as his weapon against the struggles of street life. His raw, unfiltered lyrics paint vivid pictures of the hustler's life, earning him respect both in his community and beyond.

NCS's music is more than entertainment - it's survival, it's rebellion, it's truth. From underground cyphers to commanding stages across South Africa, his hustle has never stopped. Two decades in the game have only sharpened his skills and message.

Representing the real street narrative, NCS continues to drop tracks that resonate with anyone who's ever had to hustle for their dreams. This is not just music - this is a lifestyle.
